Peruvian Chicken Recipe

This Peruvian style chicken is perfect for Spring & Summer!

Ingredients

  • Olive oil for brushing
  • 1 tbsp honey
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, crushed
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp ground cumin
  • 1/2 tsp smoked paprika
  • 1/2 tsp dried oregano
  • 1/4 tsp black pepper
  • 6 chicken drumsticks
  1. Brush air-fryer basket with olive oil.
  2. Combine honey, olive oil, garlic, salt, cumin, paprika, oregano, and pepper in a large bowl. Add drumsticks and toss to coat.
  3. Cook in the air fryer at 400 degrees for 15-20 minutes or until an instant read thermometer reads 165 degrees fahrenheit.
  4. Remove from air-fryer and serve.
